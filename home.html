What is it?

Barcamp is a free unconference with no scheduled speakers.

A 30 hour barcamp in the heart of the UK. The venue, food and drink is provided by sponsorship. The content is provided by the attendees. Bring along something cool to share with people (rules are it's brand new content, something you've never presented before). Sessions can be tutorials, presentations, photo walks, techy, crafty or something completely different!

Arrive at 10am Sat 23rd July and stay all the way through until 4pm 24th July. There will be games, hacking, drinking and fun throughout the night and sessions throughout the day.

The event is paid for by our lovely sponsors.
